From Secret Hearts #152:



WHERE TO MEET BOYS
By Page Peterson

So you want to meet boys, make new friends, and enjoy yourself?

GET ACTIVE: Open your eyes and take a fresh look at your neighborhood. Pretend this is the first time you’ve seen it—you’re a reporter and you have to gather material for an article on what the young people in your town are all about.

Enter in your valuable little notebook the times and places of all the happenings that interest you.

When does the football team practice? When does the youth group of the local drama, social or political club get together? When is the church picnic?

Even better, make yourself an “insider” and help organize some of these events. Do they need volunteers to decorate the hall for the dance? Or to pick up leaves in the park, or paint the walls of the clubhouse? You can meet more people and have more fun preparing for an event than on the day itself.

NOW: Get to talking to the boys you see all around you.

SOON: You’ll find you’ve got involved in a pleasant social whirl that begins to take care of itself.

People enjoy having you around. Your phone is busy. You’re in demand. You have become a more interesting person.
