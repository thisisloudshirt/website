“Sylvan Esso is [a] new project from Mountain Man’s Amelia Meath and Nick Sanborn of Megafaun. Their first single ‘Hey Mami’ is the perfect synthesis of both group’s sound—folksy crooning and layered harmonies from Meath and Sanborn’s crunchy synths that both bounce and stutter. It’s an unexpected sound for a song that ruminates on cat-calling, but the hybrid is enchanting.”

“A delicate song like a set of nesting dolls, Sylvan Esso’s ‘Hey Mami’ opens bare-bones with gently layered vocals, piling on claps, bells and finally some hazy electronics. It’s a subtly genius track about a girl getting cat-called, the kind of song that turns ‘Blurred Lines’ on its head with little to-do, with fragility turned strength. Listen closely, turn it up, repeat, wear it like armor.”
—All Things Go Music
